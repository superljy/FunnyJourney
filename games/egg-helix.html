Egg Helix

Guide bouncing Easter eggs to the top in this exciting arcade challenge!

Game Description

Egg Helix is an arcade game where you steer a trunk adorned with many tennis rackets. There will be an easter egg bouncing up and down. Your main purpose is to take this egg to the top. Make eggcelent steering movements to achieve it.

With its unique gameplay mechanics and colorful design, Egg Helix offers a fun challenge that will test your reflexes and coordination skills.

How to Play

Controls

  • Use your mouse to steer the trunk with tennis rackets
  • Position the rackets to catch the bouncing Easter egg
  • Guide the egg upward to reach the top of the helix
  • Avoid dropping the egg or letting it fall off the rackets
  • Make precise movements to maintain control of the egg

Tips & Tricks

  • Start with small, controlled movements
  • Anticipate the egg's bounce trajectory
  • Position the rackets slightly ahead of where you expect the egg to land
  • Be patient and maintain a steady pace
  • Focus on keeping the egg balanced on the rackets
