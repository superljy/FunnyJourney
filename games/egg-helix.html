Egg Helix

Guide bouncing Easter eggs through the twisting helix tower!

Game Description

Egg Helix is an exciting arcade game where you guide bouncing Easter eggs through a twisting helix tower. Navigate through the rotating platforms and avoid obstacles to reach the bottom safely.

With its addictive physics-based gameplay and colorful design, Egg Helix offers a fun challenge that will test your reflexes and timing skills.

How to Play

Controls

  • Use your mouse or finger to rotate the helix tower
  • Guide the egg through the gaps in the platforms
  • Avoid hitting the colored sections
  • Reach the bottom to complete the level

Strategy Tips

  • Time your rotations carefully
  • Watch the egg's bounce pattern
  • Be patient and don't rush
  • Learn from each attempt to improve
