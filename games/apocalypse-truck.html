Apocalypse Truck

Survive the zombie apocalypse with your monster truck!

Game Description

When the virus hit, cities crumbled, people vanished. The world went silent except for the groans of the undead. You're one of the few survivors of zombie apocalypse. Your only hope is a beat-up monster truck, with some spikes, plates, and just enough horsepower to push through the zombie chaos.

Navigate through the post-apocalyptic wasteland, crushing zombies and obstacles in your path. Can you survive long enough to reach safety?

How to Play

Controls

  • Arrow Keys or WASD - Drive the truck
  • Space - Nitro boost
  • Mouse - Navigate menus
  • R - Restart level

Tips

  • Crush zombies for extra points
  • Use nitro to escape dangerous situations
  • Avoid obstacles that can damage your truck
  • Collect power-ups along the way
  • Keep moving to survive
  • Upgrade your truck when possible
