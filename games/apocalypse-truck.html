🚛 Apocalypse Truck

Drive through the apocalypse and survive the zombie invasion! Navigate dangerous terrain in your armored truck.

🎮 How to Play

Use arrow keys to drive your truck through post-apocalyptic landscapes. Avoid or crush zombies, collect fuel and supplies, and reach safe zones.

🎯 Game Features

  • Post-apocalyptic driving
  • Zombie survival elements
  • Truck customization
  • Resource management
  • Multiple environments

💡 Tips & Tricks

Manage fuel carefully between checkpoints. Upgrade your truck for better survival. Sometimes it's better to avoid zombies than fight them. Look for supply caches.
