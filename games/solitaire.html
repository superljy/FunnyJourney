Classic Solitaire

The timeless card game that's perfect for a quick mental break!

Game Description

Classic Solitaire is the timeless card game that's been a staple on computers since the early days of personal computing. Sort cards by suit and rank in this engaging patience game that offers a perfect mental break from your daily routine.

With its simple yet strategic gameplay, Classic Solitaire provides a relaxing experience that can be both meditative and mentally stimulating.

How to Play

Rules

  • Build four foundation piles by suit from Ace to King
  • Build tableau columns in descending order with alternating colors
  • Move exposed cards between columns to organize them
  • Turn over cards from the stock pile when you need new cards
  • Win by moving all cards to the foundation piles

Card Placement

  • Foundation piles build up (A, 2, 3, ..., Q, K)
  • Tableau columns build down (K, Q, J, ..., 3, 2, A)
  • Tableau requires alternating colors (red, black, red, etc.)
  • Only Kings can be placed on empty tableau spots
  • Only Aces can start foundation piles
