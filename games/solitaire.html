🃏 Classic Solitaire

The timeless card game that's perfect for a quick mental break. Sort cards by suit and rank to clear the table!

🎮 How to Play

Build four foundation piles from Ace to King by suit. Use the tableau to temporarily store cards. Only Kings can go in empty tableau columns. Alternate red and black cards in descending order.

🎯 Game Features

  • Traditional Klondike Solitaire rules
  • Relaxing and meditative gameplay
  • Perfect for mental exercise
  • Score tracking and timing
  • Unlimited undo moves

💡 Tips & Tricks

Always move Aces and Twos to foundations when possible. Don't automatically play cards to foundations. Try to free up face-down cards early. Build tableau piles evenly when possible.
