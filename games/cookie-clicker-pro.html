Cookie Clicker Pro

Click your way to cookie empire domination in this addictive idle clicker!

Game Description

Cookie Clicker Pro is the ultimate idle clicker experience that will keep you hooked for hours! Start by clicking cookies to earn points, then use those points to buy upgrades, buildings, and automation that will generate cookies for you even when you're not actively playing.

With dozens of upgrades, achievements to unlock, and endless progression, Cookie Clicker Pro offers the perfect blend of active clicking and idle gameplay that makes it impossible to put down!

How to Play

Basic Gameplay

  • Click the cookie to earn your first cookies
  • Use cookies to buy upgrades and buildings
  • Purchase cursors for automatic clicking
  • Buy grandmas, farms, and factories for passive income

Strategy Tips

  • Balance between clicking and buying upgrades
  • Focus on buildings that give the best cookie-per-second ratio
  • Watch for golden cookies for bonus rewards
  • Unlock achievements for permanent bonuses
