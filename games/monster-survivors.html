Monster Survivors

Survive the relentless monster onslaught and become the ultimate survivor!

Game Description

Monster Survivors is an intense action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters in a fight for survival. As the difficulty increases with time, you must strategically collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and master your skills to survive as long as possible.

With its roguelike elements and fast-paced gameplay, every session offers a unique experience that will keep you coming back for more!

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies
  • Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters
  • Choose upgrades when you level up

Strategy Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid being overwhelmed
  • Focus on collecting experience gems quickly
  • Balance offensive and defensive upgrades
  • Learn enemy patterns to survive longer
