Poop Clicker

Game Description

Poop Clicker is the most ridiculously funny clicker game that will have you laughing while you click! This absurd and entertaining game takes the clicker genre to new heights of silliness. Click to generate... well, you know what, and use your earnings to buy upgrades that will automate your production.

Don't let the silly theme fool you - this is a surprisingly addictive idle clicker with deep progression mechanics, achievements, and endless entertainment. Perfect for when you need a good laugh and some mindless clicking fun!

How to Play

Basic Gameplay

  • Click the main button to start generating points
  • Use points to buy upgrades and automation
  • Purchase auto-clickers for passive generation
  • Unlock special buildings for massive production

Strategy Tips

  • Balance between clicking and buying upgrades
  • Focus on buildings with the best efficiency ratio
  • Watch for special bonus events and multipliers
  • Complete achievements for permanent bonuses
