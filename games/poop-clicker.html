Game Description

Poop Clicker is the most hilarious and addictive clicker game you'll ever play! Click to create chaos, earn points, and unlock crazy upgrades that will keep you laughing and clicking for hours.

With its simple yet engaging gameplay and humorous theme, Poop Clicker offers endless entertainment that's perfect for quick breaks or extended gaming sessions.

How to Play

Controls

  • Click the poop to earn points
  • Use points to buy upgrades
  • Purchase auto-clickers for passive income
  • Unlock new achievements and bonuses

Strategy Tips

  • Balance manual clicking with auto-upgrades
  • Invest in multipliers early for exponential growth
  • Complete achievements for bonus rewards
  • Watch for special events and bonuses
