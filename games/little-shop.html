Little Shop

Run your own shop and build a successful business empire!

Game Description

Little Shop is an engaging shop management simulation game where you take control of your very own retail business. Start with a small shop and work your way up to building a commercial empire by serving customers, managing inventory, and making smart business decisions.

Experience the thrill of entrepreneurship as you handle daily operations, satisfy customer demands, and expand your shop to become the most successful business in town!

How to Play

Controls

  • Mouse - Navigate and interact
  • Click - Select items and customers
  • Drag - Move items and arrange shop
  • Menu - Access shop management

Tips

  • Keep popular items in stock
  • Serve customers quickly for tips
  • Upgrade your shop regularly
  • Monitor your cash flow
  • Expand to new product categories
  • Keep customers happy for repeat business
