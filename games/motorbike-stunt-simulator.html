Game Description

Motorbike Stunt Simulator is a thrilling 3D racing game that puts you in control of powerful motorcycles on challenging stunt tracks. Perform incredible jumps, tricks, and stunts while mastering the art of motorcycle control and balance.

With realistic physics, multiple bike models, and exciting stunt courses, this simulator offers an authentic motorbike experience that will test your skills and reflexes!

How to Play

Controls

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control the motorbike
  • Press spacebar for boost and special tricks
  • Balance your bike during jumps and landings
  • Complete challenging stunt courses

Strategy Tips

  • Practice controlling your bike's balance
  • Use boost strategically for big jumps
  • Land smoothly to maintain speed
  • Learn each track's optimal route
  • Start with easier bikes before advanced ones
