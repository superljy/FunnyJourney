Pyramid Solitaire

Remove cards that add up to 13 in this challenging brain teaser!

Game Description

Pyramid Solitaire is a challenging card game where you must remove all cards from the pyramid by pairing them to add up to 13. This strategic variant of solitaire requires planning and careful thinking to succeed.

With its unique pyramid layout and mathematical challenge, Pyramid Solitaire offers a perfect brain teaser that combines luck and strategy in an engaging card game experience.

How to Play

Basic Rules

  • Remove pairs of cards that add up to 13
  • Kings can be removed alone (worth 13)
  • Only exposed cards can be selected
  • Clear the entire pyramid to win

Card Values

  • Ace = 1, Numbers = face value
  • Jack = 11, Queen = 12, King = 13
  • Examples: Queen + Ace, Jack + 2, 6 + 7
  • Use the stock pile when pyramid is blocked
