Game Description

Julie Beauty Salon is a delightful simulation game where you manage your own beauty salon! Help clients achieve their perfect look through various beauty treatments, hairstyling, makeup, and spa services. Build your reputation and expand your salon business.

With its charming graphics, variety of beauty treatments, and customer management gameplay, Julie Beauty Salon offers a relaxing and enjoyable experience for players of all ages!

How to Play

Controls

  • Click on tools and treatments to use them
  • Follow customer requests for specific services
  • Complete makeovers within the time limit
  • Earn money to upgrade your salon

Strategy Tips

  • Listen carefully to customer preferences
  • Work efficiently to serve more clients
  • Upgrade equipment for better services
  • Keep your salon clean and organized
  • Happy customers give better reviews
